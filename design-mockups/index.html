Fleet Office — design direction options

Same dashboard, same data, five design languages. Open each full-screen and judge how the daily compliance workflow would feel in it.

1 · Linear Dark

Reference: Linear
Dark-first, compact density, muted indigo accent, quiet status pills. Feels like a focused ops tool; best for long screen sessions.

2 · Enterprise Blue

Reference: Stripe Dashboard
Light, classic SaaS. Blue accent for primary actions, soft card shadows, generous radius. The safest, most familiar choice for mixed audiences.

3 · Geist Console

Reference: Vercel
Sharp corners, mono numerals and dates, bordered KPI strip, outlined status chips. Closest evolution of your current Expo monochrome system.

4 · Warm Professional

Reference: Mercury / Notion
Warm ivory canvas, borderless sidebar, larger page titles, dot-status badges. Calm and approachable; lowest visual fatigue.

5 · Operations Navy

Reference: Atlassian / enterprise ops
Navy sidebar, color-coded KPI top borders, uppercase status labels, link-styled names. Most "serious enterprise system" of the five.
