# Fleet Management v2 ŌĆö Consolidated UX/UI Review

_Code-level review synthesizing 109 findings across the Admin (`/dashboard/*`) and Pilot (`/portal/*`) portals. B767 crew/cert/leave management._

## 1. Executive Summary

Overall UX health is **moderate-with-systemic-debt**. The individual screens are functional and mostly token-aware, but the app is carrying the scars of **duplicated, parallel implementations** that have drifted apart. The dominant problem is not any single broken screen ŌĆö it's that the _same concept is built 2-4 times_ and each copy looks, behaves, and validates differently. Five cross-cutting themes account for the majority of severity:

1. **Duplicate/parallel surfaces that have drifted.** Two pilot logins (`/pilot/login` Supabase vs `/portal/(public)/login` Staff-ID), two reset-password files (one a dead AI-slop variant), two registration UIs, two RDO/SDO forms (one missing CSRF), two leave-approval surfaces (`/dashboard/leave/approve` vs `/dashboard/requests?tab=leave`), and three "Add/Edit Certification" surfaces. Each pair diverges in fields, validation, copy, tokens, and safety guardrails. This is the single biggest source of inconsistency and risk.

2. **No single form architecture.** Three incompatible form systems coexist (shadcn `Form`/`BaseFormCard`, `PortalFormWrapper`, and raw hand-rolled `<input>`/`<select>`). Required-field markers render 4 different ways, submission errors 4 ways, success states 3 ways, and submit buttons hardcode `text-white` in 8 files (breaking dark mode and diverging from `<Button>`). Sibling forms on the same portal render with different focus rings, chevrons, and heights.

3. **Token-system erosion / AI-slop.** Confirmed: `components/ui/badge.tsx` hardcodes light-mode hex with **zero `dark:` variants** (verified) ŌĆö a foundational primitive that breaks contrast app-wide in dark mode. Plus hardcoded hex in charts, roster grids, and cert progress rings; emoji used as section icons and status legends across admin; banned animations (retirement badge `animate-pulse`, kanban `rotate-3 scale-105`, dead illustrated empty-state with gradients + float, pilot-login sweeping bar). A `/login` test page ships an airplane emoji and **plaintext real-looking credentials** in production (verified).

4. **Color-only status signaling on safety-critical data.** Collapsed sidebar cert badges degrade to a color-only 2px dot (red expired vs yellow expiring ŌĆö the most safety-critical signal in the app), crew-availability numbers, roster today/weekend columns, and notification type dots all encode meaning by hue alone, violating the house rule and FAA convention. Cert thresholds also diverge from the documented red/yellow(Ōēż30d)/green (portal uses 14/60-day bands that contradict the dashboard's "30d" label).

5. **Destructive actions without confirmation, and broken/dead links.** Card-view leave approve/deny, compact-card approve/deny, pilot-registration approve/deny, and portal notification delete all fire on a single click with no confirm ŌĆö inconsistent with the codebase's own `useConfirm()` convention. Meanwhile the Support page links to 404 routes, a disciplinary enum mismatch makes severity/status badges always render gray, and a `bg-[var(--color-info-bg)]0` typo (verified) renders no background.

The remediation leverage is high: a handful of shared primitives (one `PageHeader`, one `Button`/`Input`, one required-marker, one status badge, one form wrapper per portal, one `useConfirm()` wiring) would resolve a large fraction of these findings at once.

## 2. High-Priority Issues (grouped)

### A. Duplicate / parallel surfaces that have drifted apart

- **Two pilot login flows.** `app/pilot/login/page.tsx` (Supabase email/password) vs `app/portal/(public)/login/page.tsx` (custom `an_users` Staff-ID, the documented canonical path). **Fix:** redirect `/pilot/login` ŌåÆ `/portal/login` in `next.config.js`; delete the `/pilot/*` auth pages and `components/pilot/pilot-login-form.tsx`.
- **Test login page with plaintext credentials (verified).** `app/login/page.tsx:56` ships a `text-4xl Ō£ł’ĖÅ` and `:107-114` a "Test Credentials" box with email + `Password: test123`, production-reachable. **Fix:** delete the credentials block and emoji; consolidate to `/auth/login` or guard behind a non-prod check.
- **Two reset-password implementations.** `reset-password-content.tsx` (unused, Framer spring/scale celebration, shadow-xl glow, radial-dot bg) vs the rendered flat `page.tsx`. **Fix:** delete `reset-password-content.tsx`.
- **Two registration UIs** (`app/portal/(public)/register/page.tsx` vs `components/pilot/pilot-register-form.tsx`) with different success copy and ad-hoc token families. **Fix:** keep one, standardize on shadcn `<Alert>`.
- **Two RDO/SDO request forms.** `app/portal/(protected)/flight-requests/new/page.tsx` (CSRF headers) vs `components/portal/rdo-sdo-request-form.tsx` (**omits CSRF on fetch**, lines 125-137). **Fix:** consolidate to one component that sends CSRF and keeps the roster-period preview.
- **Two leave-approval surfaces.** `/dashboard/leave/approve` (bespoke, no-confirm) vs `/dashboard/requests?tab=leave` (canonical per CLAUDE.md redirect table). **Fix:** redirect `/dashboard/leave/approve` to the unified view; delete duplicated help/empty-state markup.
- **Three "Add/Edit Certification" surfaces.** `/certifications/new` page, the dialog (`certification-form-dialog.tsx`), and `/certifications/[id]/edit` ŌĆö with different required markers, validation (expiry-after-check only in the dialog), and the `[id]/edit` route missing the unsaved-changes guard. **Fix:** one shared cert form component used by all three entry points.

### B. Form system fragmentation (`forms-inputs` / `visual-consistency`)

- **Three form architectures coexist** for the same domains; `leave-request-form`, `flight-request-form`, `pilot-register-form`, `task-form`, `disciplinary-matter-form` hand-roll raw HTML while siblings use shadcn. **Fix:** one system per portal ŌĆö `PortalFormWrapper` + shadcn primitives for portal, `BaseFormCard` + `Form*Wrapper` for admin.
- **`text-white` hardcoded on submit buttons in 8 files** (`form-wizard`, `task-form`, both pilot request forms, register, login, both disciplinary forms). Breaks dark-mode contrast. **Fix:** replace with `<Button>` (uses `text-primary-foreground`).
- **Required markers render 4 ways** (`text-destructive/70`, `text-[var(--color-status-high)]`, in-label-string ` *` with no aria, none). **Fix:** one helper `<span class="text-destructive ml-0.5" aria-label="required">*</span>`.
- **Submission-error display 4 ways** including a `@deprecated` `components/portal/form-error-alert.tsx`. **Fix:** converge on `components/ui/form-error-alert.tsx`.
- **Disciplinary forms have no Zod resolver** and emit generic "X is required". **Fix:** add `disciplinary-matter-schema.ts` + `zodResolver`; same for `action-form.tsx`.
- **Misleading certification dialog.** `certification-form-dialog.tsx` says "All fields are required" while Check Date/Notes are optional, and `onSubmit` **silently discards** Check Date + Notes (lines 188-193). **Fix:** correct the copy, add visible asterisks, and either persist or remove those inputs.

### C. Destructive actions without confirmation (`interaction-states`)

- Card-view leave approve/deny (`leave-approval-card.tsx`), compact-card approve/deny (`request-card-compact.tsx`), pilot-registration approve/deny (`registration-approval-client.tsx`), and portal notification delete (`notifications/page.tsx:114-136`) all fire on one click. Deny in the table/detail path correctly requires a mandatory reason + AlertDialog. **Fix:** route all through `useConfirm()` / AlertDialog with mandatory deny-reason; replace auto-dismissing inline alerts with sonner toasts.

### D. Color-only / FAA-convention violations (`accessibility`)

- **Collapsed sidebar cert badge** degrades to a color-only 2px dot (`professional-sidebar-client.tsx:223-233`) for the app's most safety-critical signal, with a generic `${badge} items` aria-label that drops the expired/expiring distinction. **Fix:** keep a numeric/`!` glyph and severity-specific aria-label.
- **Crew-availability numbers** in `leave-approval-card.tsx` are color-only except on the below-minimum branch. **Fix:** add explicit "OK / At minimum / Below minimum" text to every line.
- **Portal cert thresholds diverge from documented convention** (`certifications/page.tsx`): orange "Critical Ōēż14d" + yellow "Warning Ōēż60d" vs documented red/yellow(Ōēż30d)/green, and the dashboard labels the same data "Expiring ┬Ę 30d". **Fix:** reconcile thresholds and labels across dashboard and cert page.

### E. Broken rendering / dead logic

- **`bg-[var(--color-info-bg)]0` typo (verified)** at `profile/page.tsx:526` and `notifications/page.tsx:146` ŌĆö invalid class renders no background. **Fix:** `bg-[var(--color-info-bg)]/10` and a valid badge token.
- **Disciplinary severity/status enum mismatch.** Form writes `'low'|'medium'|...` / `'open'|'under_review'|...`; detail page switches on `'CRITICAL'|'SERIOUS'|...` / `'UNDER_INVESTIGATION'|...`. **No case matches**, so badges always fall through to gray. **Fix:** standardize on the DB enum for both form options and badge color maps.
- **`components/ui/badge.tsx` hardcodes light hex with zero `dark:` variants (verified).** Breaks contrast app-wide in dark mode. **Fix:** map variants to semantic tokens or add `dark:` pairs (mirror `button.tsx`).
- **`empty-state-illustrated.tsx`** ŌĆö aviation clip-art + gradients + `animate-float` (undefined in globals.css, so it no-ops) + off-token hex, and **unused** (verified). **Fix:** delete; standardize on `empty-state.tsx`.
- **Support page dead links.** `/dashboard/docs` and `/dashboard/tutorials` 404; `/dashboard/faqs` 308-redirects and drops the deep-link fragment. The entire 315-line `faqs/page.tsx` is dead behind the redirect. **Fix:** delete `faqs/page.tsx`, repoint Support links to `/dashboard/help` with valid anchors, remove/build the docs/tutorials tiles.

### F. PDF-labelled export downloads `.txt` (verified ŌĆö `microcopy`)

Both `app/dashboard/analytics/page.tsx:97` and `app/dashboard/planning/analytics-content.tsx:68` set the extension to `.txt` when `format === 'pdf'`, behind a FileText/PDF-implying button, with a success toast claiming success. **Fix:** generate a real PDF or relabel to "Export Report (TXT)" and align icon + extension.

## 4. By Dimension

- **accessibility (high concern):** Hand-rolled accordions with broken `aria-controls` (region not rendered when collapsed) and invalid/duplicate ids from raw category names; collapsed-sidebar color-only safety badge; tabs built as bare buttons with no `role=tablist`/`aria-selected` in both portals (planning, portal requests); raw forms lack `aria-invalid`/`aria-describedby`/`role=alert` that shadcn forms provide; non-keyboard "Remember me" checkbox; `AccessibleForm` infra exists but **no form uses it**; breadcrumb current-page uses misleading `role=link aria-disabled`; route-change focus manager ignores reduced-motion and yanks focus to h1.
- **forms-inputs (high concern):** Three architectures; 4 required-marker styles; discarded dialog fields; native `<select>` vs shadcn `Select` for the same choice; weaker change-password rules than reset; step validation via toasts instead of inline + focus; no autofocus.
- **visual-consistency (high concern):** Badge primitive with no dark mode; `text-white` buttons in 8 files; three color-token families in the requests/leave area; inconsistent H1 scale across both admin sub-areas; three Active/Inactive treatments for pilots (one wrongly `destructive`/red); inconsistent footer button alignment/spacing.
- **ai-slop (high concern):** Test-page emoji; emoji as iconography/legends across admin; hardcoded hex in charts/roster/cert-ring; banned animations (pulse, rotate, float, spring celebrations, sweeping bar, bouncing arrow); two empty-state systems including the illustrated slop file.
- **interaction-states (medium-high):** Multiple no-confirm destructive actions; silent failures (kanban PATCH only `console.error`, no optimistic update + full-screen block); dead `ApprovalChecklist` wired but never rendered; inconsistent skeleton vs spinner loading; no retry on cert fetch error; three success-state treatments.
- **navigation-ia (medium):** 404 Support links + dead FAQ; two leave-approval and two login surfaces; portal requests tab state in `useState` not nuqs (no deep-link/back-button); inconsistent breadcrumb presence (Support, cert new/edit, disciplinary detail).
- **microcopy (medium):** Fabricated stats ("Security Level: High", static "Database Status: Connected"), contradictory version numbers (v2.0.0 vs v2.5.0); PDFŌåÆtxt mislabel; raw enum/snake_case shown to users; inconsistent submit-button labels; emoji in error banners.
- **layout-responsive (low-medium):** 11-column requests table with no responsive/card fallback below `lg`; ad-hoc `p-8` on some renewal-planning pages causing gutter drift; pilot sidebar JS resize listener risks SSR off-canvas flash.
